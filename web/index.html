About Recipe Reader

Main Features

Recipe Readers allows users to:

  • Upload Recipes
  • Save Favoite Recipes
  • Search our collection of Recipes
  • See whats trending
  • Easy ingredient view for quick shopping
  • Use your voice to navigate the direction
  • Directions are read to you

Recipe Reader is your kitchen cooking assistant so you can follow recipies without having to stop and clean your hands!

Installing

Recipe Reader is easy to install on your android device.

All Android devices 2.3 (Gingerbread) and higher are supported

Upleading Recipes:

There are 2 ways to upload a recipe to the Recipe Reader Collection

1. Fill out the recipe form.

2. Use our allrecipes.com import tool.
